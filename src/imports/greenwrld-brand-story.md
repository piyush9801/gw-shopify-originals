Section 1

THE BEGINNING

Greenwrld began in 2020 when founder Maddy Doane started experimenting with transforming existing garments into something completely different.

What began as a creative outlet quickly turned into a deeper idea. Clothing didn’t have to be thrown away or replaced. It could be rebuilt, reworked, and given another life.

While studying at UCLA and competing as a Division I track and field athlete, Maddy began developing the early pieces that would eventually become Greenwrld.

⸻

Section 2

MADE AGAIN

Greenwrld is built on the idea that clothing can be reimagined instead of discarded.

Every piece begins with existing garments sourced secondhand and reconstructed by hand. What once existed as something ordinary becomes something entirely new.

The result is clothing that feels expressive, personal, and impossible to mass produce.

⸻

Section 3

THE FIRST PIECE

The earliest designs started with a simple question.

What if clothing could be completely transformed instead of replaced?

Greenwrld began with experiments. Existing garments were cut apart, reworked, and rebuilt into pieces that felt entirely new.

Those early experiments became the foundation of the brand.

⸻

Section 4

REWORKED

Every Greenwrld garment begins with materials sourced secondhand.

Instead of producing new textiles, existing garments are disassembled and reconstructed into something completely different.

This process reduces textile waste while creating pieces that feel original and unexpected.

Because the materials are sourced secondhand, each piece carries its own character.

No two pieces are exactly alike.

⸻

Section 5

MOMENTS

Greenwrld quickly began gaining recognition for its approach to reconstructed garments.

Highlights include:

• New York Fashion Week 2022 — Presented a collection of 10 looks on the runway
• Nike x Billie Eilish Sustainable Showcase — Created a four-piece collection using secondhand Nike materials
• Stanley Brand Collaboration at NYFW
• Urban Outfitters Title 9 Anniversary Collaboration
• Flying Solo New York capsule collection
• ComplexCon 2024 & 2025 vendor alongside brands like Nike, Adidas, and Bape

Greenwrld pieces have also been worn by Billie Eilish, Noah Schnapp, Post Malone, and Ronnie 2K.

Section 6

ONE OF A KIND

Each Greenwrld piece begins with existing garments.

Because the materials are sourced secondhand and reconstructed by hand, no two pieces are exactly the same.

What begins as something ordinary becomes something completely different.

Every piece is one of a kind.
